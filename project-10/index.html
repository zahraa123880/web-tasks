Flowers are available in nature in many colors and types, also it has been hybridized some of them to get the roses in distinctive and beautiful colors. Bouquets of roses are often the first choice to be presented as a gift on different occasions . The meanings offered by roses vary according to their colors and types,where each color of roses is used to express specific feelings towards the person to whom it is presented. Yellow roses refers to the feelings of jealousy on the beloved, and is often used to express a change in the nature of feelings towards someone.
